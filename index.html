What Is GrabIt?

GrabIt is a platform designed to help save people more time. It allows users to send out their daily errands for people in the area to accept for a small - large sum, depending on the requester. Targeted to just the GTA, by opening your account, it will display all of the available tasks, with details of drop-offs or meet up locations. It's as easy as that!gy
